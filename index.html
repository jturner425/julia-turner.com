About

I am a PhD student in Human Development and Social Policy at Northwestern University. My research focuses on the economics of higher education, student financial aid, and how public policy shapes access to postsecondary education.

Prior to Northwestern, I was a Pre-Doctoral Research Fellow at Blueprint Labs at MIT, working with Josh Angrist, David Autor, and Amanda Pallais. I hold a B.A. in Economics and English from Amherst College.

I have served as a Staff Economist at the Council of Economic Advisers and as a PhD Intern at the Office of the Chief Economist at the U.S. Department of Education.

I am a 2025–2026 Dissertation Fellow at the Consumer Finance Institute at the Federal Reserve Bank of Philadelphia, and an NSF Graduate Research Fellow (2021–2024).
